# 📖 Pricing & Business Glossary

This glossary defines the core financial and commercial terms used throughout the Pricing Engine project to ensure alignment between Sales, Marketing, and Data teams.

* **Margin Leakage:** The unauthorized or undocumented loss of potential profit margin, typically occurring when sales representatives grant ad-hoc discounts below the corporate approved pricing floor to close a deal.
* **Gross Margin (%)**: The percentage of revenue that exceeds the Cost of Goods Sold (COGS). Formula: `(Revenue - COGS) / Revenue`.
* **Markup:** The percentage added to the cost price of goods to determine the selling price. Formula: `(Selling Price - Cost) / Cost`. *(Note: This project strictly uses Gross Margin for strategic decisions, not Markup).*
* **Price Walk (Waterfall):** A visual analytical method used to track how a starting base price is reduced step-by-step by various discounts, rebates, and leakage down to the final realized pocket price.
* **Landed Cost:** The total price of a product once it has arrived at the buyer's door. It includes the original price of the product from the factory, all transportation fees, customs, duties, taxes, insurance, and currency conversion.
